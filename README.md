## What I'm Building Next
 
**Nigerian Multilingual Conversational AI**
 
Most Nigerian AI projects wrap an English LLM and call it localized. That is not localization — it is translation lag with extra steps.
 
I am building voice-in, voice-out conversational AI that natively understands Yoruba, Igbo, Hausa, and Nigerian English — not as translated inputs, but as primary languages. The target user is someone who has never touched a keyboard. A grandmother in Enugu who wants to ask about her finances. A trader in Kano who needs business information in Hausa.
 
**The technical path:**
- Fine-tuning ASR models (Whisper + MMS) on Nigerian audio corpora
- Multilingual LLM training on Yoruba/Igbo/Hausa text datasets
- Building on and beyond the YarnGPT foundation for TTS
- MLOps pipeline for low-latency inference on Nigerian infrastructure
**First milestone:** A conversational finance tracker in Pidgin English and Yoruba, connected to Nigerian bank APIs — deployable in 2026.
 
> *This is the problem I am spending the next 3 years on.*
