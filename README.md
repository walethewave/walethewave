## What I'm Building Next

### Nigerian Multilingual Conversational AI

Most "Nigerian AI" projects wrap an English LLM and call it localized. That is not localization — it is translation lag with extra steps.

I am building **voice-in, voice-out** conversational AI that natively understands Yoruba, Igbo, Hausa, and Nigerian English — not as translated inputs, but as primary languages.

**The target user:** Someone who has never touched a keyboard. A grandmother in Enugu asking about her finances in Igbo. A trader in Kano getting business information in Hausa.

**Technical path:**
- Fine-tuning ASR (Whisper + MMS) on Nigerian audio corpora
- Multilingual LLM training on Yoruba/Igbo/Hausa text datasets
- Building beyond YarnGPT for native-language TTS
- MLOps pipeline optimized for Nigerian infrastructure constraints

**First milestone:** Conversational finance tracker in Pidgin English and Yoruba, connected to Nigerian bank APIs — deployable 2026.

> *This is the problem I am spending the next 3 years solving.*
